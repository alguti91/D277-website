About Eugene

Nestled in the Willamette Valley, Eugene is known for outdoor recreation, a love of the arts, and its youthful, innovative spirit.

  • Population: 176,654
  • Year Incorporated: 1862
  • Region: Southern Willamette Valley
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $58,500 (state median: ~$65,000)
